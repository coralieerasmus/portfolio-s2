In person user-research

We conducted user-research using post-its. We wrote down all elements of our project, then asked students to sort them based on the categories of homepage, indoor activities, outdoor activities and filters.

User-research using Forms

We got the most input from our potential user's throught the use of Google Forms. Here we asked many questions relating to our project like "What is usually your budget when going out?" and so on.
