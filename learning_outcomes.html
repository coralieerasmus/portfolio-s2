PROFESSIONAL SKILLS

FUTURE-ORIENTED ORGANISATION

For a given assignment you with guidance make a planning (timing & part-tasks) with your team members. You make agreements about the expected quality. And while working on the implementation you take into account the consequences of your chosen solution for the people and organisations involved.


I relate this learning outcome to 2 projects I have done so far, these being momento and Mario & Luigi's Pizzeria In these two projects I had to work in teams, where we had to do everything together. From planning to task division, while also maintaining clear communication with eachother. And in both of these teams that I have worked in I can say they have both been successful. I think this is because in both everyone had some of their ideas implemented in the final product and and everyones opinions were respected and discussed between all members. An example of this is when choosing to cut features from the projects.

PERSONAL LEADERSHIP

You notice and seize opportunities. You motivate yourself. You take responsibility for your actions. You work result-oriented on your assignments. You make considered choices in your study programme. You recognize learning needs in yourself, are open to feedback, and reflect on this.


This learning outcome has played a big roll in all projects I have done so far. As I have been able to complete all of them, and the reason being is that I was able to motivate myself and push through even when I would be stuck on a code for an extended period of time. I would frequently ask teachers for feedback on my codes to make sure I was on the right track, and would take the feedback I received from teachers and some students and add those into the project.

INVESTIGATIVE PROBLEM SOLVING

You remain curious and ask questions throughout the solution process. You answer questions by using an appropriate approach: pragmatic, critical and based on sources.


The momento project works perfectly with this learning outcome. Because through the whole process of creation we used it in many ways. We had many user-research based interactive prototypes. We asked other classmates their opinions on our design.

TARGETED INTERACTION

You take the direct stakeholder into account when working on an assignment. You pay attention to what you want to communicate and in which shape you do this. You recognize tasks within the group work, and take on your role within the group. You address others about their behaviour and role within the group.


Projects that fit this description best are momento and Mario & Luigi's Pizzeria in all projects the direct stakeholders are the teachers, therefore any feedback we got we tried our best to implement into the final product.
